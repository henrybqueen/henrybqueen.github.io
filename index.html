II

Roots of Unity. This is what came first to mind. The graph of the polynomial \(f(z)=|z^2-1|^2\). This is a polynomial: \[ \begin{align} f(z) &= |(x+iy)^2-1|^2 \\ &= |(x^2-y^2-1)+i(2xy)|^2 \\ &= (x^2-y^2-1)^2 + (2xy)^2. \\ \end{align} \] That is, f is a real polynomial of two real coefficients \[ f(x, y) = (x^2-y^2-1)^2 + (2xy)^2. \]
